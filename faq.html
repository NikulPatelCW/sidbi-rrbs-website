Common questions about our initiative

Find answers to common questions about the SIDBI–RRB Co-Lending Arrangement, the partnership framework, and how it supports MSME lending.

What is the SIDBI–RRB exclusive Co-Lending Program?

The SIDBI–Regional Rural Banks (RRBs) Co-Lending Program is a financing initiative where both institutions jointly provide loans to eligible MSMEs. Under this arrangement, the loan exposure is shared equally (50:50) between SIDBI and the partnering RRB, helping improve access to credit for businesses.
