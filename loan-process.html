A 100% digital loan journey process

From registration to repayment, we have streamlined the process in 7 simple steps to make your lending experience personalized and fast.

1. Registration

Register on our digital platform to start your loan application. Our on-screen guidance and tutorial videos help you complete the journey smoothly.

  • Complete online form
  • Upload business documents
  • Watch tutorial videos

2. PRE Decisioning

Your application is evaluated through our Digital Business Rule engine. The decision is conveyed to you instantly on the platform.

  • Automated evaluation
  • Credit assessment
  • Instant decision notification

3. Field Visit

A field verification visit is conducted using our field visit application. Relevant information and data are captured digitally.

  • Physical verification
  • Business assessment
  • Data capture vis app

4. Sanction

Key Fact Statement (KFS) is generated and accepted on the platform. Sanction letter and draft documents are shared via email.

  • KFS generation
  • Sanction letter issued
  • Draft documents shared

5. Documentation

Complete the loan documentation digitally through Aadhaar-based e-sign. No physical paperwork required.

  • Digital document
  • Aadhaar-based e-sign
  • No physical paperwork

6. Disbursement

Request loan disbursement through the platform. Funds may transfer to your account through our secure online process.

  • Requested disbursement
  • Online fund transfer
  • Instant processing

7. Repayment

Register on our digital platform to start your loan application. Our on-screen guidance and tutorial videos help you complete the journey smoothly.

  • Payment gateway
  • eNACH auto-debit
  • Download statements
