What is this SIDBI–RRB co-lending initiative?

This is a joint initiative where SIDBI and Regional Rural Banks work together to provide structured and affordable loans to MSMEs. SIDBI brings credit expertise and refinancing support, while your local RRB manages the relationship and application process — so you get the strength of both institutions.
